Meridian, Idaho

Location

Meridian is located in Ada County, Idaho. It takes up a total of 26.84 square miles and sits just west of Boise.

About

History

Meridian was initially established in 1891, growing from a small settlement to a larger town after establishing irrigation and connecting with the railway system.

Population

Meridian's current population sits at about 139,740 people.

Geography

Meridian is located on a flat plain within the Treasure Valley. The Snake River runs to the south of the city and the Boise River runs to the north.
